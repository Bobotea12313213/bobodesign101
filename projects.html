Product Design| Butterfly Stool

Project description:Mindful Meds strives on delivering a flawless and intuitive experience in medication adherence for older persons, especially those within vulnerable circumstances such as early stages of dementia. Mindful Meds is a Internet Of Things (IoT) based ecosystem of products that become an integrable part of an elderly persons life in regards to keeping them in the loop of their medication taking routines. This ecosystem consists of the main automatic medication sorting machine with two reminder products disguised into everyday house items such as a keepsake cabinet and flower vase to mitigate stigmas related to owning and using medical product / systems. The reminder system communicates through integrated memo-glow technology which enables an ambient lighting approach to notify users in a subtle and unobtrusive method.

Hydro-lamp | Product Design

Project description:In this inventive project, I embarked on the task of designing a hydro-controlled lamp that utilizes syringes and small water pipes for all lamp movements, with the main body of the lamp crafted from cardboard. The concept was to merge sustainability and innovative engineering, showcasing how everyday materials could be repurposed for unique and practical applications. The lamp's movement and adjustability were entirely powered by water, with the syringes serving as hydraulic pistons. Manipulating the syringes allowed users to control the lamp's orientation, providing both function and aesthetics. The use of small water pipes for fluid transfer emphasized the simplicity of the hydraulic system.
